Features

Select the items from the list. Bid against both time and others

Every item has got its own time limit. Once the time limit has been reached, bidding is closed

The bidder with highest bidding amount is notified and also the seller

The bidding process:

  1. Register as a user (A user can buy or sell on the platform)
  2. Log in using the registered details.
  3. check the product catalog and note the time your desired item goes live on auction
  4. come back at this time adn place your bid
  5. Monitor the various bids placed and adjust accordingly. (every item will be on auction for 5 minutes tops)
  6. Bid on as many items as you like. (On any bid that you win, The item will eb available for you to pay within 24 hours.)
  7. All won bids will be on you profile page on the items won section.
